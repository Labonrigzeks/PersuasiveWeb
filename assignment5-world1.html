The Plane Crash of Flight PS752

08.01.2020 at 06:12 local time (02:42 GMT), Ukraine International Airlines (UIA) flight PS752 took off from Tehran's airport, Imam Khomeini Inernational Airport (IATA). Before the plane leaving the airport's air space, the plane crashed. Iran Civil Aviation Organization (CAO.IR) has the upmost priority to investigate and explain the crash.

The involved plane model of Ukraine International - Boeing 737-800

The reason for the plane crash

There has been a cause of relentless amount of speculations on social media, as for the cause of the plane crash. The truth is, the plane was shot down unintentionally by our missiles, our operator had mistaken the plane for a cruise missile. We lied to society, by saying the plane suffered techincal problems shortly after take-off, as we thought it would stir more problem around the society. We believe, our decision was right, to come out with the truth at a later date.

Statement from President Hassan Rouhani

Newscaster translating President Hassan Rouhani's statement written in English to Farsi speaking language

Our President is deeply sorry for the downing of the plane. Because of the threats that were occurring at the time and the potential chance of the Americans attacking, the Iranian armed forces were ready to step up in the case of an attack by the Americans. Unfortunately, because of human error, by believing that there were American attack inbound, the rocket was fired which hit the target we thought was an "cruise missile". The government apologizes deeply.

The conclusion

On behalf of The Government of the Islamic Republic of Iran, we give our condolences to the families involved and the whole nation of Iran. There is no form for right justice that can be executed. The only action we can take, is to learn from the mistake that was done and prevent it from ever happening again, if such situation appears at a later date. We hope the nation of Iran forgives us.
